# Information Theory

A personal knowledge base about the theory and practice of organising, transmitting, and retrieving information. Maintained by Jakob ([@soenderby](https://github.com/soenderby)).

The scope spans several intersecting fields: information theory (Shannon), communication theory (Luhmann), network theory (Granovetter), classification and indexing (Hjørland), cybernetics (Ashby, Wiener, Beer), and epistemology/strategy (Boyd). The connecting thread is: **how do systems — biological, social, mechanical, cognitive — organise, transmit, retrieve, and create information?**

## Purpose

Two purposes, both honest:

1. **Practical.** Inform how I organise and retrieve information in my own projects — particularly [ai-resources](https://github.com/soenderby/ai-resources), a curated knowledge base about AI and agents that serves as a working experiment in information organisation with AI assistance. As that collection grows, new techniques and methods may be needed at different scales.

2. **Curiosity.** This is a subject that fascinates me. The theoretical foundations of information, communication, and classification are deep and surprisingly interconnected.
